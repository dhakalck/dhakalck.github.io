About Me

I hold a Ph.D. in Econometrics and Quantitative Economics with specialized expertise in simulation modeling (Markov, microsimulation) and economic evaluation for non-infectious diseases. My work bridges the gap between rigorous quantitative research and practical public health applications.

Mission: Driven by a commitment to translate research into policy to improve the lives of those affected by chronic diseases.
